Invenio Developer Forum

Almost every Monday afternoon (at 4pm CEST) we meet physically and virtually to discuss some interseting topics around the development of Invenio. Everyone is welcome!


You can join these forums from any computer through our videoconference setup. Here the steps:

  1. Install the videoconferencing software: Vidyo.
  2. Join our virtual room.

Invenio User Group Workshop

Next IUGW will take place in Heinz Maier-Leibnitz Zentrum (MLZ) the 21-24 March 2017.

Join us!

We meet with all the Invenio users, developers and administrators from around the world, more or less, once a year. We call these events Invenio User Group Workshop. Usually, the workshop consists of a series of lectures, practical exercises, and discussions with Invenio developers. The goal is to enable a better understanding of Invenio features and capabilities, to discuss specific needs, forthcoming features and developments.
